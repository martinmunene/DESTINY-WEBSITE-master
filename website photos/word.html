Act 20:28 Take heed therefore unto yourselves, and to all the flock, over the which the Holy Ghost hath made you overseers, to feed the church of God, which he hath purchased with his own blood.

NO WEAPON DESPITE THE SOURCE CAN BREAK THE COVERING OF THE BLOOD OF JESUS

Rev 12:11 And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of their testimony; and they loved not their lives unto the death.

  • THE BLOOD OF JESUS IS SUPREME I.E WHAT IT SAYS IS FINAL
  • IF THERE IS PRECIOUS THING THAT WE ARE GIVEN IS THE BLOOD OF JESUS CHRIST
  • THE DEVIL WAS DEFEATED THROUGH THE COVENANT BLOOD OF JESUS CHRIST
  • THE ARE MANY BLOOD BUT THE BLOOD OF JESUS IS ABOVE THEM ALL

    • WHY DO WE NEED THE COVERING OF THE BLOOD OF JESUS ?

    1.WE ARE LIVING IN EVIL DAYS
    Eph 5:15 So be careful how you live. Don't live like ignorant people, but like wise people.
    Eph 5:16 Make good use of every opportunity you have, because these are evil days.
    Eph 5:17 Don't be fools, then, but try to find out what the Lord wants you to do.

    2.SO THAT YOU WILL NOT BE EXPOSSED TO THE AGENDAS OF THE ENEMIES
    Exo 12:13 The blood on the doorposts will be a sign to mark the houses in which you live.
    When I see the blood, I will pass over you and will not harm you when I punish the Egyptians.
    Gal 6:17 From now on let no one cause me trouble, for I bear on my body the marks of Jesus.

    3.BECAUSE YOU MAY NOT BE AWARE OF THE HIDDEN WEAPONS OF THE ENEMIES TO ATTACK YOU

    4.TO SPEAK GOOD THINGS IN YOUR LIFE
    Heb 12:24 You have come to Jesus, who arranged the new covenant, and to the sprinkled blood that promises much better things than does the blood of Abel

    2.BELIEVE IN THE COVERING OF THE BLOOD OF JESUS
    =MAY BE YOU BELIEVE IN YOUR GATES,MONEY, WISDOM ETC
    3.SPEAK ABOUT THE COVERING TIMES
