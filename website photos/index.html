Destiny Word Lifestyle

We Trust in God: Our faith, trust, and hope is in Christ. God’s Word guides our decisions, refreshes us, and creates an unshakable faith. Our dependence on God is reflected in our commitment to prayer.

Making Disciples as Jesus commanded

We help you reach others for christ and help them grow and multiply disciples. The goal of the website is to enable the multiplication of disciples.

The power of the Cross

Jesus on the cross, bearing our sins, gives us power to die to sins. Further, we are enabled by the power of the cross of Jesus to live for righteousness

Church participation

We encourage participation in church and offering your time and talents which improve and strengthen both you and the people around you

Our main church departments

Destiny word tv is Under Wonders Tabernacle Church - Mwihoko Which has the following departments

Choir department
Assists in leading congregational singing. As choir members trained in music and voices, we seek to encourage others to participate in worship
Sunday School department
The lessons of Sunday school are prepared in such a way that they emphasise the main Christian doctrines and practices
Worship department
We place greater importance on serving others than playing music, being noticed, and having a good time
Ushering department
Ushers set the spiritual climate of the service by presenting a joyful attitude, make others feel welcome and comfortable.
Hospitality department
They conduct outreach activities, sponsoring events that welcome new members, and promoting fellowship throughout the organization.
Youth department
Investing in today’s youth is necessary in growing the body of Christ. Teaching young people in the church to grow in their relationship with the Lord prepares them to serve Christ in all they do.
